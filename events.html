Speaking


  • Debate

    Debate


    "It is better to debate a question without settling it than to settle a question without debating it." This January, ready your argumentative skills and convincing powers as Verba Maximus brings to you the ultimate debate competition: an exhilarating event full of fierce arguments and popular topics . Present your cases skillfully and engage in fiery rebuttals to sway the public opinion in your favour and experience the adrenaline rush as you take on this electrifying debate.

  • JAM

    JAM

    You'll have to shoot down the speaker before your opponents can, blabber as much as is humanly possible before you get interrupted. Keep an eye on the clock and, all the while do not forget, Mod is God.

  • Devil's Advocate

    Devil's Advocate

    Be prepared to venture into the realm of the morally grey and the utterly outrageous, as you attempt to defend a conventionally unpopular cause. Who's the vilest of the all?

  • Group Discussion

    Group discussion

    A healthy discussion with 8 voices trying to gain dominance over one another. Most logical one wins. With a wide variety of prompts, this event is sure to test your ingenuity and logic.

  • VM Slam

    VM Slam

    Perform spoken word poetry in a bid to mesmerise a live audience and a panel of experienced judges. 'Empty out your heart', as you drench every word in emotion.

  • Lit Quiz

    Lit Quiz

    A quiz filled with literature trivia and facts, with questions covering everything, from classics to comics.

  • Youth Mahasabha

    Youth Mahasabha

    The Hindi debating event gives you chance to take the stage, and present your arguments as you discuss a variety of topics.

  • Bamboozled!

    Bamboozled!

    Inspired from the famous sitcom F.R.I.E.N.D.S, but with a few unique twists, find the 'Joey' to your Chandler as you compete in teams of two in one of the wildest game shows ever.
